Classic Skillet Lasagna

Description

A comforting, deeply savory classic made entirely in a single skillet to save you from washing a dozen pans. It delivers all the rich, layered flavors of a traditional baked lasagna—saucy beef, creamy ricotta, and molten mozzarella—in half the time.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) lean ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 small yellow onion, finely diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 8 lasagna noodles, broken into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and diced onion, cooking until the meat is browned and the onions are translucent. Drain excess fat.
  2. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the marinara sauce and water, stirring to combine. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  4. Add the broken lasagna noodles, pressing them down so they are mostly submerged in the sauce. Cover the skillet with a lid, turn the heat down to low, and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes until the noodles are completely tender.
  5. Remove the lid. Drop dollops of ricotta cheese evenly over the top of the noodles.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan uniformly over the entire skillet.
  7. Cover with the lid for another 2-3 minutes until the mozzarella is fully melted and bubbling. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.
